# References

## Core Papers for Evolutionary Image Approximation
1. Karl Sims (1991), *Artificial Evolution for Computer Graphics*  
   https://www.karlsims.com/papers/siggraph91-backup.html
2. Berg et al. (2019), *Evolved Art with Transparent, Overlapping, and Geometric Shapes*  
   https://arxiv.org/abs/1904.06110
3. Li et al. (2020), *Differentiable Vector Graphics Rasterization for Editing and Learning* (DiffVG)  
   https://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~tzli/diffvg/
4. Hirschorn et al. (2023), *Optimize and Reduce: A Top-Down Approach for Image Vectorization*  
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.11334
5. Liu et al. (2025), *Bezier Splatting: Differentiable Inverse Vectorization*  
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.16424
